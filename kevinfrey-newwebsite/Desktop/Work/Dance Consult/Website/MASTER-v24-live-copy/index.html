Our Mission

At 1:1 Dance Consult, we believe that creative professionals deserve careers that are both artistically fulfilling and financially sustainable—without sacrificing their individuality, vision, or wellbeing.

Our mission is to empower artists with the perspective, tools, and systems to build careers that reflect who they truly are.

We bridge the gap between artistic excellence and business success through personalized mentorship, practical strategy, and cutting-edge AI integration—all built on a foundation of trust, privacy, and creative freedom.

Christos Tsiantoulas

Pro Dancer + Choreographer

Christos is a dynamic multi-hyphenate—dancer, choreographer, creative director, and educator—who turned self-taught roots into a thriving international career. Jenna Shortt
