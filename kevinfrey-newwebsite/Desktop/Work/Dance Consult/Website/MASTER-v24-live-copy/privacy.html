This Privacy Policy describes how Kreative Fruition Inc., doing business as 1:1 Dance Consult (“we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, and protects personal information obtained through our website, booking platforms, and consulting services.

By visiting our site or engaging in our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1:1 Dance Consult is a creative consulting and mentorship platform owned and operated by Kreative Fruition Inc., a registered corporation based in the United States. All client communications, transactions, and data are processed under the legal entity Kreative Fruition Inc.

2. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our services.

Personal Information may include:

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Payment information (processed through secure third-party providers such as Stripe or Calendly)
  • Session-related materials or notes voluntarily shared by clients

Non-Personal Information may include:

  • Browser type, IP address, and device information
  • Website analytics and cookie data used to optimize performance

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Schedule, deliver, and improve 1:1 sessions and services
  • Communicate session details, resources, or updates
  • Process secure payments and issue receipts
  • Analyze site traffic and improve user experience

We will never sell or rent your personal information to any third party. Data is only shared when necessary to complete transactions or maintain our service operations (e.g., Calendly, Stripe, Google Workspace).

4. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information. Access to personal data is limited to authorized members of the Kreative Fruition team and verified consultants.

All payment data is encrypted and processed through secure third-party systems compliant with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Correct or update your information
  • Request deletion of your data (except where retention is required by law)
  • Withdraw consent for communications or marketing

Requests can be made at any time via hello@1on1consult.org.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve functionality, personalize experiences, and gather analytics. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site performance.

7. Data Retention

We retain client data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, and maintain business records. Session notes and related materials are stored securely and may be deleted upon written request.
